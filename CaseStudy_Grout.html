Outdoor Afro Summary

Outdoor Afro celebrates and inspires black connections in nature. Thye do this through outdoor education, recreation, and conservation.

Positive Impacts

Inspires people to engage with nature. Encouraging black participation in nature increases awareness about the significance of nature and removes the privileged status that access to nature currently has.

Potential Drawbacks

Does not address systemic issues of climate destruction in neighborhoods that are predominately black and brown
